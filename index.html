OUR SERVICES

Evergreen Electrical provides full residential and commercial electrical services. With over ten years of experience you can count on us to see your electrical project through from start to finish while clearly communicating with you throughout the process. Our services include but are not limited to the following:

  • NEW HOME WIRING

    We are equipped to help you navigate the many decisions that come along with wiring a new house or cottage. By working closely with you throughout the process, we’ll make sure you get the end product you desire.

  • RENOVATIONS

    Proper lighting and a well planned electrical layout are some of the most effective ways to make the most of your renovation. Let Evergreen Electrical help you take your project to the next level - no job is too big or too small.

  • INSTALLATIONS

    From changing out light fixtures and ceiling fans, upgrading to new receptacles and switches to wiring up your new hot tub, Evergreen Electrical can get the job done. We stay up to date on the newest technologies and can help you pick out the best products to suit your needs.

  • TROUBLESHOOTING AND GENERAL MAINTENANCE

    Receptacles not working? Lights flickering? Mystery switch that doesn’t seem to do anything? Whether it is an issue of safety or convenience Evergreen Electrical can help you figure out what is going on and get your electrical system working safely and effectively for you.

  • ELECTRICAL SERVICES AND SERVICE UPGRADES

    Whether it is a new electrical service for your new home or cottage, a temporary service to your new lot still in development, or a panel upgrade from that old fuse panel, Evergreen Electrical can take care of it for you.

  • GENERATOR INSTALLATIONS

    Evergreen Electrical can take care of all of your backup power needs. Whether it is just a few essential circuits you need to energize when the power goes out or a full automatic whole home stand by generator we have you covered.

  • EV CHARGER INSTALLATIONS

    Electric vehicles are one of the fastest growing industries today. If you need a charger for your new electric vehicle at your house or if you are a business that would like to provide your customers with this option, give us a call and we can set you up with whatever you require.

  • SOLAR PROJECTS

    Evergreen Electrical, along with our partners at Sunfish Solar, can help with any solar project big or small. Whether you are wanting to become more independent and go off grid or wanting to sell your power back to the utilities we can help to design a system that meets your specific needs.

  • AND MORE...

    Have an electrical project in mind that's not listed? Reach out and we can see if it's a good fit.

ABOUT US

    Evergreen Electrical is owned and operated by Master Electrician Spencer Tackaberry. Spencer was born and raised on the Saugeen (Bruce) Peninsula and now lives near Lion’s Head with his young family. He started the business in 2021 after working for over 10 years as an electrician in the area. Evergreen Electrical is built on the foundation of high quality work, and consistant communication. We take the time to develop an open and honest relationship with our clients so there are no surprises at the end of the project.

    We stay up to date on all of the newest technologies in the industry so that we can provide our clients with the best knowledge and products available to them. At Evergreen Electrical we are passionate about doing our part to help take care of the planet. We keep this in mind with all of our projects and are excited to be partnering with Sunfish Solar to offer our clients with industry leading knowledge and materials for all of our solar projects.
